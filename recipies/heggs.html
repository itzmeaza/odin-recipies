WE GOT EGGGGGSSSSS

A simple, yet high in protein delicacy that will make turn your office into a gas chamber from your flatulance.

    What you need for this:
  • Lots of eggs (I recommend farm fresh, but make sure they are washed)
  • A solid sturdy pot
    How To Basic (jk)
  1. Go to your sink and put water into your trusty pan
  2. Wait for the water to boil (pro-tip! Add some salt to it to make it boil quicker!)
  3. Once the water is boiling, gently place WASHED eggs into boiling water
  4. Once eggs start floating, remove heat and let them cool
  5. Peel back the shell, salt and pepper lightly, and enjoy!
