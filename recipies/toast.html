Who doesn't like plain, unbuttered toast?

Ahhh, a delicacy across the world. Give this a go when you're barely alive enough to consider going to work as your next nutritious meal!

    What you need:
  • A loaf of Wonderbread Toast for a dollar
  • A stove
  • Vegtable Oil (instead of butter, for comedy sakes
  • A pan (because I'm a monster who expects you to make toast on the stove)
  • (Please don't actually follow this recipie)
    How To:
  1. Get your Wonderbread out, get 2 slices because that's standard.
  2. Put your pan on the stove and set it to medium heat
  3. Wait for the pan to get warm, then place the fluffy, bio-engineered bread onto the pan
  4. Wait for about 1 minute, then if you're with it, you don't need tongs or a spatula to flip it! Use your hands (disclaimer, please don't)
  5. Wait another min, then throw that hot sucker on a plate (throw it hard)
  6. Spread some good ol' crisco on that sucker. Lay it on THICK
  7. Eat and enjoy!
