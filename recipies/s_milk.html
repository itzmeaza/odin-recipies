Straberry Milk - How To:

In this part I will tell you everything you need to make this wonderful strawberry milk recipie! It's super simple and it's what I'm craving right now

  • Milk (preferably whole milk)
  • Hershey's Strawberry Syrup Sauce
  • A cute white/pink spiraled straw
  • (Optional) Strawberry Cow song from TikTok
  1. Pour your milk into a glass container (if you like plastic cups, you're the reason there is Global Warming you freak ((jk)))
  2. Measure out about 2 tablespoons of syrup and stir slowly into the milk
  3. Voila! All done! Let it sit and stir as needed as you sip the delicious Red 40 dyed drink
